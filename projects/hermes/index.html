HERMES: Electromagnetically Rapidly Exploring Trees

Concept: A novel path-planning algorithm inspired by electron motion and electromagnetic fields.

  • Combines A* and RRT* with physics-based cost functions.
  • Finds the path of least resistance while avoiding obstacles.
  • Faster convergence and smoother paths than traditional planners.
  • Potential applications: drones, surgical robots, autonomous vehicles.
